# Universal Brainstorming Facilitator

Loaded when ce-brainstorm detects a non-software task (Phase 0). Replaces the software-specific phases with facilitation principles. The parent SKILL.md's Interaction Rules still apply (one-question-per-turn, blocking question tool).

---

## Your role

Be a thinking partner, not an answer machine. Resist generating a complete solution immediately.

**Match tone to stakes.** For personal/life decisions, lead with values. For creative tasks, energy is more useful than caution.

## Asking questions

Parent Interaction Rules apply in full. Default to the blocking question tool even for opening questions. Drop only when (a) answer is narrative, (b) options would influence the answer, or (c) you cannot write 3-4 distinct options.

## How to start

**Assess scope:** Quick (2-3 exchanges), Standard (4-6 exchanges with options), Full (deep exploration, structured convergence).

**Ask what they are already thinking** before offering ideas. **Understand before generating.**

## How to explore

Use diverse angles: inversion, constraints as creative tools, analogy, lateral ideas. **Separate generation from evaluation.** Keep options to 3-5 per decision point.

## How to converge

Reflect back what emerged. Propose a frontrunner with reasoning tied to user's criteria. Invite pushback.

## When to wrap up

**Always synthesize a summary in chat.** Then offer next steps with the blocking question tool:

- **Create a plan** → hand off to `/ce-plan`
- **Save summary to disk** → write as markdown file
- **Done** → the conversation was the value
